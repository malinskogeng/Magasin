essential

An indispensable accessory, the Small Book Tote appears in jute canvas in the Dior Fall 2022 women’s collection by Maria Grazia Chiuri. The motto “L’union fait la force” (Strength through unity), recalling that of the factory of Christian Dior’s father, is embroidered in 3D, an ode to the ateliers’ virtuoso savoir-faire. An object of desire blending heritage and a contemporary vision.
